How private are these tools?

Very. The numbers you type and the files you upload never leave your browser - all calculation and PDF generation happens locally. We don't have a server-side database for any of this: no signup, no account, no cloud sync. Your vendor list and recent documents save to your browser's localStorage only. The one caveat: anonymous page analytics (which page you viewed, not what you typed) fire if you accept the cookie banner, and not otherwise.

No. Every tool here is free, browser-based, and requires no email or account.

Nowhere. Each tool runs entirely in your browser. Saved vendors, customer lists, and recent documents live in your browser's localStorage on your device. We have no server-side database for any of these tools.
