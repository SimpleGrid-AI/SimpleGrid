Why NPS works for manufacturing

NPS strips away survey complexity - one question, one number, anyone can compute. The reason it works is the asymmetry of word-of-mouth: in B2B, a single referral from a satisfied customer drives more new revenue than 100 outbound emails. NPS captures the "would they refer?" propensity in a way that customer satisfaction scores don't. The cutoff at 9 is deliberate - 7s and 8s feel positive but rarely actually refer. Only 9s and 10s do the work for you.

How to actually use NPS - it's the follow-up, not the score

The score itself is just diagnostic. The value comes from the follow-up. Promoters get asked for referrals and testimonials - this is your cheapest growth channel. Detractors get called personally - this is your cheapest retention move. Passives get asked an open-ended "what would have made it a 9 or 10" - that's your next product improvement list. Track NPS by customer, by salesperson, by region; you'll see your strengths and weaknesses faster than any other tool.
