The hidden costs every ERP TCO needs to capture

Internal resource time. 1,000+ hours of your team's time during implementation is normal for mid-market. At $80/hour loaded, that's $80,000 vendors never mention. Customization. The vendor's "standard" rarely matches your shop floor. Modifications start at $150/hour and run 6-12 months past go-live. Data migration. Your data is messier than you think; cleanup is the #1 cause of timeline slip. Ongoing customization budget. Software needs adjustment every quarter. Budget $25K-$75K/year minimum. Annual maintenance. For perpetual licenses, 18-22% of license cost every year - forever.
