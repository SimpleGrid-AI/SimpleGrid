AQL Sampling Calculator (ANSI/ASQ Z1.4) - the right inspection sample for the right risk.

Inspect 100% and you'll spend forever - and you'll still miss defects. Inspect 5 and you'll let bad lots through. Z1.4 (formerly MIL-STD-105E) is the global standard. Enter lot size, criticality, and supplier history - get sample size, accept, and reject numbers.
