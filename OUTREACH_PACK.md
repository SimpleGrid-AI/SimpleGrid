# Outreach Pack (link-building via the free tools)

Internal doc (excluded from publishing). The audit found ~zero referring domains. The 35 free calculators are the most linkable asset SimpleGrid owns (they help anyone, no signup, no pitch). Pitch them as free resources, not as a product.

## Why this works
Manufacturing associations, trade blogs, and ops newsletters constantly need to link to "a free [X] calculator." SimpleGrid has 35 of them with PDF export and no gate. That is a clean, value-first ask that earns editorial links and AI-citable mentions.

---

## Email template A — resource pitch (cold, to a blog/newsletter editor)

> **Subject:** A free [job cost / OEE / reorder point] calculator for your readers
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I run SimpleGrid, where we build custom ERP for US manufacturers. Along the way we built a set of free tools our customers kept asking for, and we made them public, no signup or email gate.
>
> Given your piece on [specific article/topic], this one might be useful to your readers: [tool URL]. It does [one-line: e.g. "true job cost + margin + a suggested price, with a PDF export"].
>

>
> Either way, thanks for the work you publish.
> Mukund
> Founder, SimpleGrid

## Target-type list (research the specific names/contacts before sending)

**Manufacturing associations / public programs**
- NIST MEP (Manufacturing Extension Partnership) state centers — each state has one, most have a resources page.
- NAM (National Association of Manufacturers) regional chapters.
- SME (Society of Manufacturing Engineers).
- Local/state manufacturing associations (e.g. CMTC California, WMEP Wisconsin, etc.).
- SCORE / SBDC manufacturing mentors (resource pages link freely to free tools).

**Vertical trade bodies (match to the calculators)**
- Furniture: AHFA (American Home Furnishings Alliance), WMIA.
- Apparel: AAFA (American Apparel & Footwear Association).
- Metal fab: FMA (Fabricators & Manufacturers Association).
- Food production: trade groups per sub-vertical.

**Ops / manufacturing blogs & newsletters**
- Lean/CI blogs, MES/ERP-adjacent blogs, plant-management newsletters.
- Small-business finance blogs (for the EBITDA/SDE/valuation/exit tools — these reach the owner-operator selling the business).
- M&A / business-broker blogs (valuation, SDE, exit-readiness, customer-concentration tools are perfect for them).

**Tool-by-tool angle (which tool fits which audience)**
- Valuation/exit cluster (business-valuation, ebitda, sde, exit-readiness, customer-concentration, revenue-per-employee) -> business brokers, M&A advisors, exit-planning blogs.
- Compliance cluster (iso-9001, osha, quality-inspection, insurance) -> safety/quality consultants, EHS blogs.
- Costing cluster (job-cost, oee, burden-rate, landed-cost-adjacent) -> manufacturing ops blogs, MEP centers.
- Generators (invoice, quote, PO, BOM, production-schedule) -> small-manufacturer how-to blogs, "free templates" roundups.

## Process
1. Pick 20 targets/week with a real resources page or recent relevant article.
2. Personalize the first line (cite their specific page).
3. Send template A or B.
4. Track replies; add any earned link's domain to a running list.
5. Every earned editorial link is a referring domain the audit said you have ~zero of.
