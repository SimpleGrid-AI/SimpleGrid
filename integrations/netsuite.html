FAQ

NetSuite & SimpleGrid, in short

Do we keep using NetSuite?

Yes. NetSuite stays your book of record - SimpleGrid runs operations on top and feeds it, so finance never re-keys.

Will it create duplicate customers or items?

No. Records are matched to your existing NetSuite customers, vendors and items during setup.
